Shipping Policy

Thank you for choosing Devengers Vault as your preferred online toy store. We want to ensure that your shopping experience with us is as seamless and enjoyable as possible. Please review our shipping policy to understand the terms and conditions related to shipping and delivery of your orders.

Shipping Destinations:

We currently offer shipping to addresses within the Philippines. If your location is not included in the list, please contact our customer support team, and we will do our best to assist you.

Processing Time:

After placing an order, please allow us [Processing Time] to process and prepare your order for shipment. We make every effort to dispatch orders promptly. However, please note that during peak periods or special promotions, processing times may be slightly longer. We appreciate your patience and understanding.

Shipping Methods and Delivery Times:

We partner with reputable shipping carriers to ensure reliable and efficient delivery of your orders. The available shipping methods and estimated delivery times will be displayed during the checkout process. Please note that delivery times may vary depending on your location and any unforeseen circumstances that may impact the shipping carrier's operations.

Shipping Charges:

Shipping charges are calculated based on the total weight, dimensions, and destination of your order. The shipping cost will be displayed during the checkout process, and you will have the opportunity to review and confirm the charges before proceeding with the payment.

Order Tracking:

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email containing a tracking number and instructions on how to track your package. You can use this tracking number to monitor the progress of your shipment on the shipping carrier's website.

Customs and Duties:

For international orders, please note that customs duties, taxes, and any additional fees imposed by your country's customs authorities are the responsibility of the customer. These charges are not included in the order total or shipping cost and may be collected upon delivery. Customs policies vary from country to country, so we recommend contacting your local customs office for more information.

Shipping Restrictions:

Certain products may be subject to shipping restrictions due to their nature, size, or destination regulations. If any restrictions apply to your order, we will notify you promptly, and alternative arrangements or cancellation options will be provided.

Incomplete or Incorrect Addresses:

Please ensure that you provide accurate and complete shipping information, including the recipient's name, address, and contact details. We are not responsible for any delays or non-delivery caused by incorrect or incomplete addresses provided by the customer.

Lost or Damaged Packages:

In the unfortunate event that your package is lost or damaged during transit, please contact our customer support team immediately. We will work with the shipping carrier to investigate and resolve the issue as quickly as possible. Please refer to our "Returns and Refunds" policy for more information on how to report and handle damaged or missing items.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our shipping policy or require assistance with a shipping-related issue, please contact our customer support team. We are here to help and ensure that your orders are delivered to you in a timely and satisfactory manner.

Note: The above shipping policy is subject to change at our discretion. Please refer to the most recent version available on our website for the most up-to-date information.

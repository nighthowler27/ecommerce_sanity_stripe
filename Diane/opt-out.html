    Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide certain functionalities.

    • Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow our website to remember choices you make, such as your language preference or your login information. They enhance the functionality and personalization of our site.
    • Advertising and Targeting Cookies: We may use cookies to deliver personalized advertisements to you based on your browsing behavior and interests. These cookies may collect information about your online activities, such as the pages you visit and the links you click.
